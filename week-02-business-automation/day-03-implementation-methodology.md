## Purpose of Implementation Methodology

UiPath devised the implementation methodology to provide a **common roadmap for implementation teams across the globe**.

Ensures consistency, efficiency, and best practices in automation delivery worldwide.

---

## Four Common Implementation Models

### Model 1: Internal CoE
- **Client:** Department within organization
- **Provider:** Internal Center of Excellence (CoE)
- **Team:** Internal implementation team with UiPath expertise

### Model 2: External Partner
- **Client:** Organization seeking automation
- **Provider:** External automation services provider (typically UiPath official partner)
- **Team:** Partner's implementation team

### Model 3: UiPath Professional Services
- **Client:** Organization seeking automation
- **Provider:** UiPath directly
- **Team:** UiPath Professional Services implementation team
- **Includes:** Licenses + implementation services

### Model 4: Hybrid Model
- **Client:** Organization seeking automation
- **Providers:** Both UiPath AND partner
- **Team:** Combined implementation resources

## Client Team Roles

The client team typically includes dedicated professionals (not all roles required for every project):

**1. Engagement Sponsor**
- Dedicated functions crucial for project delivery

**2. Client Project Manager**
- Actively engages with Implementation Team

**3. Process Owner**
- Domain expertise and process authority

**4. Process SME (Subject Matter Expert)**
- Deep process knowledge and validation

**5. Client IT Team**
- Technical infrastructure and support

**6. Support Team**
- Ongoing operational support

**7. Automation Operations Manager**
- Day-to-day automation management

## Key Success Factors

### Clear Role Definition
> "A team with clearly defined roles and responsibilities makes a project effective."

### Effective Role Performance
> "For an automation project to be successful, each role must perform its responsibilities effectively."

### Active Client Engagement
- Client team actively engages with Implementation Team
- Collaboration across budget, time, and scope constraints

---

## Key Takeaways

**Implementation Success Requires:**
- Understanding methodology framework
- Clear roles and responsibilities
- Proper team structure (both implementation and client sides)
- Active collaboration between teams
- Adherence to standardized processes

**BA Role in Implementation:**
- Bridge between business and technical teams
- Ensure requirements are properly captured
- Facilitate communication across stakeholders
